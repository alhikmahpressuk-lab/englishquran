About this website

This website is being built as a modern and thoughtful home for The English Qur’an, bringing together beautiful presentation, clear navigation and faithful verse-by-verse access.

Readable for today

The aim is to make the Qur’an easy to approach, easy to navigate and easy to continue reading, while keeping the presentation dignified and serious.

Arabic and English together

Every surah page is intended to place the Arabic verse before the English verse, with numbering in both Arabic and English for consistency.

Professional and uncluttered

The design is clean and restrained, giving the reader a calm experience that feels modern, organised and worthy of the Qur’an.
