About this translation

This website follows the manuscript of The English Qur’an and presents the translation within a clear and disciplined reading environment.

The importance of this translation

This translation was produced in response to a pressing need, to present the Qur’an in English that is faithful, precise, intellectually honest, and still natural and readable for the modern reader. It serves readers who seek guidance but struggle with archaic language, younger generations raised in English speaking settings, and thoughtful non Muslims who wish to approach the Qur’an without unnecessary barriers.

Translation method and conventions

The method is simple and disciplined, to let the Qur’an speak without commentary, embellishment, or distancing language. Wherever possible, the translation remains close to the Arabic wording. Where English requires adjustment, it does so carefully and transparently. The aim is faithful communication, clarity, and direct engagement with the Qur’anic voice.

About the translator

A. K. Khan is a UK based Islamic educator, author, and researcher whose work is devoted to restoring direct and intelligible engagement with the Qur’an for modern English readers. His background combines early Qur’anic study, advanced English academic training, and sustained study of classical Arabic, allowing him to approach Qur’anic translation with clarity, discipline, and fidelity to meaning.

Mission and vision

The mission of this work is service, service to the reader, service to understanding, and service to the Qur’an itself by conveying its message clearly without unnecessary intrusion. This website also stands within a wider vision, by Allah’s permission, to expand this work into every language so that more people across the world may approach the Qur’an with clarity, seriousness, and trust.
