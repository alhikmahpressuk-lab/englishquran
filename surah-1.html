Surah 1

Al-Fātiḥah

The Opening

From The English Qur’an, translated by Dr A K Khan

Arabic name
الفاتحة
Verses
7
Revelation
Makki
بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ ۝١
1.
In the name of Allah, the Most-Merciful, the Ever-Merciful.
ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ ۝٢
2.
All praise belongs to Allah, Lord of all worlds.
ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ ۝٣
3.
The Most-Merciful, the Ever-Merciful.
مَٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ ۝٤
4.
Master of the Day of Judgement.
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ ۝٥
5.
You alone we worship, and You alone we ask for help.
ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ ۝٦
6.
Guide us to the straight path,
صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ ۝٧
7.
the path of those upon whom You have bestowed favour, not of those who have incurred anger, nor of those who have gone astray.
